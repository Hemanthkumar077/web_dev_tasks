About the Anime

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba is a Japanese anime based on the manga by Koyoharu Gotouge. It follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is killed by demons and his sister Nezuko is turned into one. The anime features stunning visuals, emotional storytelling, and intense battles, especially those involving the elite Hashira.
