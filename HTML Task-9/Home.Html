5. What is the difference between div and span?

div is a block-level element used for grouping larger sections. span is an inline element for styling small parts of text.

7. What are attributes in HTML?

Attributes provide extra information about elements, like href, src, alt, id, and class.

8. What is the iframe tag used for?
